About me

Hello! My name is Shaine, and I’m from Meitar. I hold a B.Sc. in Industrial and Management Engineering from Ben-Gurion University. I’m currently a student in a Full-Stack Development bootcamp, and this personal introduction page is part of today’s lesson as we learn the basics of HTML and CSS in the web development section of the course. I’m very enthusiastic to learn, improve every day, and be an active part of this bootcamp community as I build my skills and create real projects.

My Interests

I’ve always enjoyed learning topics that involve logic, algorithms, and problem-solving, especially when there’s room for creative thinking. That’s one of the reasons I’m so excited about coding — it’s a great mix of structure and imagination.

Since we’re learning CSS in this part of the course, I’m also realizing I really like the design side of building websites. I’m not a professional designer or anything — it’s just something I’ve always liked in everyday life (for example, noticing what looks good in interior design and how small details can change the whole feel of a space). Now that I’m starting to build websites and web applications, I’m excited to bring that same interest into making things look clean, aesthetic, and user-friendly.

Outside of tech, I love hanging out with friends and family — going out, touring around, and spending quality time together. And lately, I’m also learning cooking at the same time, so I really enjoy trying out new dishes with friends when we’re together.

My Goals

My main goal in this bootcamp is to make the absolute best use of the opportunity and reach a high professional level as a developer. I want to build strong skills and real confidence so I can contribute to meaningful projects — including projects my dad works on (he’s a software developer), which would be really exciting for me. At the same time, I’m focused on creating solid career opportunities for myself and becoming competitive in the job market.

Short-term goals

In the short term, I’m aiming to stay consistent and do every task — including this personal intro page and the upcoming assignments — with diligence and sincerity. I also want to go beyond the minimum requirements whenever I can, learning extra concepts for personal enrichment and long-term growth.
